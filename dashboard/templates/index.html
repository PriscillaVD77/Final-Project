Movie Analysis: Fresh or Rotten?

In this project, we made it our goal to predict the outcome of movies. We targeted critic reviews through the movie database Rotten Tomatoes. The movie industry has been constantly revolving since its emergence. They are acessable on the big screen and from the touch of our hands but what does it take for them to be worthy of a good review? With the movie industry severely impacted by COVID and constantly adapting film makers and directors need to look at all angles to decide if a movie will be worth making. Will this movie be a success in the eyes of the critics?

We have created a machine learning model that will use the features chosen with your input below to help predict your prospective movie will have a fresh or rotten review. Input your movie title and details and submit for your prediction!
