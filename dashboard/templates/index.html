Movie Analysis: Fresh or Rotten?

Is it possible to consistently predict the opinions of movie critics based on information about a movie? In this project, we made it our goal to predict the Rotten Tomatoes status of a movie and whether it will be rated Fresh or Rotten.

The movie industry has been constantly evolving since its emergence. Movies are accessible on the big screen and from the touch of our hands, but what does it take for them to be worthy of a good review?

With the movie industry severely impacted by COVID and constantly adapting, film makers and directors need to look at all angles to decide if a movie will be worth making. Will this movie be a success in the eyes of the critics? Movie theater owners are also trying to make decisions about which movies to show in their theater, as better rated films could attract a larger audience.
